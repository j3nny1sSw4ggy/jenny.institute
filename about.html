WHAT IS JENNY INSTITUTE?

A Community

We aim to bring a calm, casual and chill environment to help members learn, ask, and collaborate without pressure, or intimidation. 

A Movement

The world is in a spot of stagnation. Knowledge is no longer free, information is pay-walled, and gate-kept, and it is crippling us. Knowledge should be free, knowledge should be accessible, and information should not be withheld from us.

A Place to Learn

Not only do we want to help you learn on your own, but we want to help you be taught, by people who are just as passionate as you, and are truly qualified to help you succeed in your educational goals. 

A Place to Grow

Our model is built around the idea that your growth, is our growth. We want to pride ourselves on quality people, who have the aspiration and the dedication to grow, educationally and beyond.
